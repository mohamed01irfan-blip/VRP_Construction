About VRP Constructions

VRP Constructions is a DTCP (Roc.No.274/2024/TD1) registered construction company transforming the dreams of people in Theni since 2019. We are committed to delivering high-quality construction at affordable costs.

We offer a wide range of services, from construction consulting to complete execution of civil and interior works, catering to different customer needs and budgets.

Our team consists of creative architects, experienced civil engineers, skilled structural engineers, and dedicated laborers. Together, we ensure high standards in quality construction, timely project delivery, and maximum customer satisfaction.

With reasonable pricing and excellent quality, VRP Constructions has successfully completed more than 80+ projects and earned the trust of hundreds of satisfied customers across Theni, Madurai, and Tenkasi.

Today, we are recognized as one of the highest-rated and most recommended construction companies in Theni.
